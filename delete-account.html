Delete your GDG account

You can delete your GDG account directly inside the GDG app.

Steps

  1. Open the GDG app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

When your account is deleted the following data are permanently removed:

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Username
  • Contacts

If you need help contact support.
